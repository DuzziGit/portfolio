Nebulite

"Nebulite" is a pixel-art journey on an alien world. Control a quirky alien, gather tiered resources, earn upgrades, and manage oxygen in this top-down escapade.

  • Platform: Web (Playable on itch.io)
  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Team Size: 3
  • Role: Lead Designer/Developer
  • Duration: 72 hours
  • GitHub: Nebulite Repository
Play
  • Details

Key Features:

  • Oxygen Management: Balance exploration with survival.
  • Progressive Resource Gathering: Work your way through 5 tiers of resources.
  • Day-End Earnings: Review daily resource collection and earnings.
  • Strategic Upgrades: Boost abilities and equipment for deeper exploration.
  • Central Spaceship Hub: Manage resources and track progression.
  • Mineral Belts System: Strategically mine within defined rings around your spaceship, each revealing a different tier of resources.
